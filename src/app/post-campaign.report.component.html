Page Performance
Page View The number of times a web page was viewed. A page view is counted on every page load (this includes reloads and back buttons). One visit contains one or more page views.
Landing by Email Campaign* Click-throughs represent the number of times that a tracking code for a given campaign was passed into reporting
Total Visitors The number of unique people that access your site within a given time period (e.g. day, week or month).
Total Visits Any number of Page Views in one “session.” A visit begins when a person first views a page and ends after 30 minutes of inactivity (Web analytics general industry standard). One unique visitor could make multiple visits during a given time period.
Visits from Email Campaign # of visits from an email campaign within a given time period. When a visitor clicked on an email link that has been tagged with an external tracking code, system will recognize this visitor during 30 days if cookies are not disabled or deleted in the meantime.
Visitors from email Campaign # of visitors from an email campaign within a given time period
Bounce Rate (overall) A bounce is counted when a visit consists of one page-view, meaning that the entry page and exit page are the same page. The bounce rate is bounces divided by entries.
Bounce Rate (from email) A bounce is counted when a visit consists of one page-view, meaning that the entry page and exit page are the same page. The bounce rate (from email) is bounces divided by entries only for email campaign.
Unique Form Submissions # of contacts that submitted forms independent than email campaign
Unique Form Conversion Rate (overall) # of Unique Form Submissions / Total Visitors
